# Essays

Analytical works **about** the Aevyra system. These are not part of the canon itself,
but explore, formalize, and extend the ideas introduced in the Books.

## Feyra Formula Series

A cycle of articles by Saeluth (with co-authors) providing mathematical analysis
of freedom, parasitism, traces, and triadic dynamics within the Aevyra framework.
